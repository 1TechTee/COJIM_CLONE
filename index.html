About Us

God, Jesus Christ and the Holy Spirit are the spiritual leaders of the City of Jesus International Ministry through God's servant Christopher Orji

Ever since the City of Jesus International Ministry was founded in 2019, it has been a place of worship, prayer, and fellowship for believers. We are committed to spreading the gospel and making disciples of all nations as commanded by our Lord Jesus Christ.

WHAT ARE YOU DOING TO THE BODY OF CHRIST?

During a powerful Sunday service at the City of Jesus International Ministry, the Spirit of God moved mightily through His servant, Gods Servant, Christopher Orji. In a heartfelt moment, Miss Tonia, a dedicated chorister in the ministry, was divinely located and blessed with the sum of 500,000. The joy and emotion on her face were undeniable. This act of divine generosity reminds us that God sees, remembers, and rewards faithfulness. This is Love in Action.

LOVE TO THE ELDERLY

During a Sunday service, the Spirit of God once again moved through His anointed servant, Christopher Orji, as Prof. Tseayo, a respected elder in the ministry, was located and honoured with a cash gift of 1,000,000. The congregation rejoiced as this remarkable act of love unfolded before their eyes. Prof. Tseayo, overwhelmed with gratitude, received the blessing with humility and visible emotion, a testament to the faithfulness of God and the reward of steadfast service.

LESS PRIVILEDGED OUTREACH

In a recent outreach organized by the ministry, orphans and the less privileged were blessed with food items, clothing, financial support, and words of encouragement. The outreach, led by the man of God himself, was not just a distribution of material goods, but a divine visitation filled with prayers, comfort, and the tangible love of Christ. Smiles lit up the faces of children who had once known sorrow. Mothers and guardians wept tears of joy, not just for the help received, but for the hope restored. Pure religion and undefiled before God is this: to visit the fatherless and widows in their affliction James 1:27

Faith Rewarded: From Sacrifice to Supernatural Supply

Despite facing serious financial hardship, a devoted member of City of Jesus International Ministry still honoured his responsibility to God in his own small way. During a live Sunday service, the Spirit of God led Prophet Christopher Orji to locate and bless him with 1,000,000 a divine reward for his faith and sacrifice. His story is a powerful reminder that God sees the heart and honours faithfulness, even in lack.
